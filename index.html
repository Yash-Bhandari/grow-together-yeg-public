Grow Together YEG

Edmonton's new zoning bylaw will help us fight climate change, lower taxes and keep the city affordable.

Financial Sustainability

Edmonton loses money on every new suburb that's built. If we encourage new housing in areas where infrastructure already exists, we can bolster the city's revenues and help keep property taxes low.

Learn More

Climate Action

Increasing density is the most impactful thing a city can do to reduce its GHG emissions. The environmental benefits of higher density infill housing far outweighs its cost - it's not even close.

Learn More

Affordability

Housing shortages have caused rents to skyrocket nationwide. Permitting middle density housing in more places will boost the supply of housing and help keep rents low.

Learn More

About Us

Grow Together YEG is a coalition of everyday citizens advocating for a more sustainable, vibrant and affordable Edmonton.

Our members are not employed in the development industry and we do not receive any funding from developer groups.
